Habesha Style

Women's dress

This is the kind of dress worn by women from Eritrea or Ethiopia. However this is only worn on special occasions like Easter, christmas or other holidays.

Men's dress

Men's dress come in different styles and designs. The most common one is traditional suit,However sometimes they can also come in fasionable designs.

Childeren's dress

Children can also have stylish and beautiful clothes. Girls and boys have different kinds of clothes.

Coffee Ceremoney

Coffee Ceremoney is part of the habesha culture. It is a means of gathering for people to catchup. It normally starts with roasting the cofee beans and let the guests smell it.

Normal Food

Habesha dishes are really delicious. they are served with Injera and different kind of souces. Raw meat is also served with chili souce, don't be shocked, it's fresh meat.

Vegan Food

There are also plenty of vegan food choices. One thing though if you go to someone else's house and they offer you food it's rude to say no. So be ready to eat wherever you go.
